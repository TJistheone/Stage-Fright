The Band
Jake Rivers
| Lead Vocals

Jake grew up in a small coastal town, where the sound of waves and the hum of his dad’s vinyl records sparked his love for music. Known for his soulful voice and heartfelt lyrics, Jake is the band's emotional core. When he’s not writing songs, you’ll find him surfing or journaling in a worn leather notebook he’s had since high school. His favorite quote: “Music is what feelings sound like.”

Henry Blake
| Lead Guitarist

Henry is the wildcard of the group, known for his intricate solos and love for experimenting with sound. Born into a family of classical musicians, Henry rebelled by picking up an electric guitar at age 13 and never looked back. His playing style blends blues, rock, and a touch of funk, making him the band's creative spark. When he’s not shredding, he’s probably sketching designs for his dream custom guitar.

Thomas Hayes
| Bass Guitarist

Thomas is the quiet, steady presence that keeps the band grounded. A self-taught bassist, he started playing in his high school jazz band before falling in love with rock music. Hes a tech geek at heart and often tweaks the bands equipment for the perfect sound. Outside of music, Thomas loves photography and has a knack for capturing serene landscapes. Fun fact: he names all his bass guitars after movie characters.

Cole Benett
| Drummer

The heartbeat of the band, Cole brings an infectious energy to every performance. A drummer since the age of 8, Cole honed his skills playing in garage bands and local punk shows. He’s the joker of the group, always quick with a laugh or prank, but his passion for rhythm is no joke. Cole spends his downtime skateboarding and collecting vintage vinyl. His motto: “Play loud, live louder.”
